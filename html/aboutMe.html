    Chatelaine

I was awarded a lordship (a prestigious award) for my service during this time.

    Corresponder

    I served as a corresponder for IWOC (Incarcerated Workers Organizing Committee). A corresponders duty is to read the emails sent to the org and respond accordingly or pass the message to those needed or relay information and resources and distribute our newsletters. we would get over a 1000 emails a week to sift through, often having to read through tragic events.

My duties included making executive decisions on behalf the student body, represent Mid-State at public events, and serve as extra hands for on campus events. With being a student representative I also received additional leadership training.
